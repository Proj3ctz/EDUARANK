About Us

EDURANK was conceptualized to support institutional decision-making through objective academic performance metrics. It aggregates teaching effectiveness, research output, service contributions, and student feedback.

The system is tailored for San Beda College Alabang, aligning with its commitment to excellence, innovation, and evidence-based evaluation.
